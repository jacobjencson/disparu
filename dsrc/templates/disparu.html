Welcome to Disparu!

We are conducting a search for failed supernovae, the literal dispappearance of a massive star upon collapse into a black hole, with the Hubble Space Telescope. We obtained a set of new HST/ACS images over 41 orbits of a sample of nearby galaxies with decades of archival coverage to search for these dissapearing stars.

Our Team: Jacob Jencson (Science and development lead), David Sand (Program PI), Phil Daly (Technical Support), Nathan Smith (Science Team), Jennifer Andrews (Science Team), Jeniveve Pearson (Science Team), Jay Strader (Science Team), Stefano Valenti (Science Team)
